designbuild

I’m a front-end web developer and digital user interface designer living in New Orleans, Louisiana. I love creating engaging & intuitive user experiences, utilizing innovative design techniques to build responsive websites and mobile applications.*

*Okay, okay, I realize that sounds like a lot of web jargon... To put it plainly:
I'm obsessed with creating digital experiences that people genuinely enjoy using.
So take a look at my portfolio, head over to my blog for web dev insights, and contact me to discuss a project, a full-time position, or to just talk shop.
I look forward to working with you!
