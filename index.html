Who am I?

“I am a front-end web developer and digital user interface designer living in New Orleans, Louisiana. I love creating engaging & intuitive user experiences, utilizing innovative design techniques to build responsive websites and apps.”*

* Okay, I know that's a lot of web jargon... to put it plainly:
I'm passionate about making digital experiences that people genuinely enjoy using.
So take a look at my portfolio and please don't hesitate to contact me to discuss a project, a full-time position, or to simply talk shop!
